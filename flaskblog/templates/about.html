Welcome!


Learn Chinese Together is an application for students to learn the Mandarin Chinese language. Students will be able to do fun learning activities that help them understand Mandarin Chinese from an English perspective.

A feature to this application that will be heavily emphasized will be the interaction between students. In order to progress through the lessons in the application, students will have sessions where they need to interact with other students using the application in Mandarin. These directed conversations will better help students gain Mandarin Chinese fluency. In addition to this, there will also be forums available for students to ask questions and discuss topics related to the Mandarin language.
